I'm a PhD student at UC San Diego. My research interests include development, environmental economics, and political economy.


Before I started my PhD, I worked as a Research Fellow at the Inter-American Development Bank Research Department, as a consultant at the Poverty and Inequality Platform at the World Bank, and as a Research Assistant at the Universidad del Rosario Department of Economics and the Central Bank of Colombia.


I hold an M.A. in Economics from Universidad del Rosario and a B.A. in Economics from Universidad Nacional de Colombia.
